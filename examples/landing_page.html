Wow sounds cool, but how are you providing any value?

A) Uni-Fund

5% of sales + 1% of future royalties [Illustration]

The Unibotz fund has two clear missions:

1. Support NFT artists: by collecting the most promising pieces from up-and-coming and established artists with the goal of helping them kickstart their careers or make it as a full time artist.

2. Scale the Tezos economy: We’d like to onboard new artists, collectors, and technologists into the Tezos chain.

The fund will continue to operate in the future as its own entity and could kickstart adittional projects.

We got some of the best tezos artists, collectors, and promoters in charge of the fund! We really do! check out our team below

B) Donation to [ORG] that is aligned with our values. [Illustration]

We want to make a big impact out of the gates. That is why we will donate 5% of the launch earnings to an organization aligned with our values.





THERE ARE THREE TYPES OF UNIBOTZ:

The Stakerz: make sure your gas fees stay low! The Stakerz fight those using non-efficient techniques like proof of work and try to make proof of stake the norm.

The White Hatz: work day and night finding scammers and hackers! The White Hatz make sure your NFTs stay in your wallet and take down the copy minters.

The Connoizzeurs: spot art masterpieces before anyone else! Scanning thousands of NFTs per second, they make sure revolutionary art is not wasted.
